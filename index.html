How It Works

Three simple steps to a compliant PDF. No accessibility specialist required.

01

Upload & Analyze

Drop your PDF in. RemeDocs scans for structural issues, missing tags, unreadable pages, and accessibility gaps against WCAG 2.1 criteria.

02

Automated Remediation

The engine applies structure tagging, runs OCR on scanned pages, generates AI-powered alt text, corrects reading order, and sets document metadata.

03

Download & Review

Receive a remediated PDF with a full compliance report. Human-in-the-loop review is available before finalizing.
