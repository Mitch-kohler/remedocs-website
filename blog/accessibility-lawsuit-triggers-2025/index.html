The Most Common Accessibility Lawsuit Triggers in 2025 — And How to Avoid Them

Jan 14, 2026 · 9 min read

ADA web accessibility litigation has grown every year since 2017. In 2024, over 4,200 federal lawsuits were filed — the highest on record. Plaintiffs' firms use automated scanning tools to find violations and file demand letters at scale.

Why PDFs are the #1 target

PDFs are everywhere and almost universally inaccessible. Inaccessible PDFs are straightforward to identify programmatically — download, validate, capture errors. Most cited violations: missing alt text (78%), untagged documents (71%), missing document language (54%), inaccessible forms (49%), incorrect reading order (38%).

🛡️ The best defense is documented remediation. A paper trail showing systematic action and conformance is often enough to resolve complaints before litigation.
